# Harmonic Relationship System Guide

## What Are Harmonics?

Harmonics are special frequency relationships between dimensions that unlock bonuses and reflect the deeper physics of resonance-based dimensional travel.

## How to Discover Harmonics

1. **Tune two or more dimensions** to create specific frequency ratios
2. **Listen for the chime** - Each harmonic type has a unique sound
3. **Receive the bonus** for 2 seconds after detection

## Tips for Discovery

1. **Use Q key** to quickly query target frequencies
2. **Tolerance is 2%** - You don't need perfect precision
3. **Harmonics last 2 seconds** - Reapply for continuous bonuses
4. **Multiple harmonics stack** - Try dim 1-2 octave + dim 3-4 fifth!
5. **Works across any dimension pair** - Not just 1-2

## Mathematical Cheat Sheet

For any base frequency `f`:
- **Octave:** `f * 2.0`
- **Perfect Fifth:** `f * 1.5`
- **Perfect Fourth:** `f * 1.333`
- **Golden Ratio:** `f * 1.618`
- **Major Third:** `f * 1.25`
