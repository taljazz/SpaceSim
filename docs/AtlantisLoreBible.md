## How to use this document

This is a **faithful compilation of what the sources claim and channel** — it records what each
tradition *says* about Atlantis and attributes it. It is **not** a fact-check of whether Atlantis was
real or whether the technologies worked. For a sim built to carry resonant tones to its players, the
*claims* are the raw material; their literal truth is beside the point.

Two things to hold the whole time you read:

1. **There is no single "Atlantean canon."** The only *ancient* source is Plato (~360 BCE), and Plato
   has **no crystals, no frequencies, no consciousness-tech, and no flight** — just a moral parable
   about a naval empire drowned for its hubris. Everything this sim runs on (the Tuaoi crystal, the
   realms, Solfeggio tones, the Merkaba, the Halls of Amenti) comes from a **modern esoteric synthesis**
   built between ~1880 and today, layered on top of Plato's skeleton.
2. **So the real test of "does it fit the lore" is not "is it ancient" — it's "is it consistent with
   the synthesis we've chosen, and is it spoken in that world's vocabulary."** This document tags every
   major thread with its **source stream** and whether it is *ancient-attributed* or a *modern overlay*,
   so you can build wisely.

The document has five parts:
- **Part I — The Atlantis Bible (in-world canon):** drop-in lore, by domain.
- **Part II — The Source-Stream Map:** who said what, and how credible/old each stream is.
- **Part III — The Frequency Provenance Table:** which tones are ancient-attributed vs modern (critical
  for an audio sim that makes claims through sound).
- **Part IV — Where the streams agree and conflict:** the timeline and fall-cause divergences.
- **Part V — Mapping the lore onto the sim.**
- **Sources** consulted (this pass; a verbatim-quote/citation pass is still owed — see the note at the end).

# PART I — THE ATLANTIS BIBLE (in-world canon)

*Written as the Atlantean record. Each section closes with a sourced provenance note and a sim hook.*

## 1. Origins & Cosmology — the Children of a Younger Sun

Before the fall there was the Law of One: the knowing that all things are one thing, that the All is a
single vibration sounding itself into countless forms, and that a soul is "a star bound to a body" —
a fragment of the Source learning its way home. The Atlanteans were not the first people; they were
the inheritors of Lemuria (Mu) before them, and they were a *race of the mind awakening* — the fourth
great wave of souls to wear flesh. In their dawn they lived close to the One: they thought not in cold
chains of logic but in living images, remembered across lifetimes; they spoke mind to mind; they read
the light. The earth itself was younger and softer then — the air hung dense and luminous as mist, the
waters thin and obedient, and the veil between the seen and the unseen was thin enough to step through.

> **Provenance.** Root-race framing (Lemuria → Atlantis as the **4th Root Race**, "first fully human,"
> intellect and speech awakening) is **Theosophy** — H.P. Blavatsky, *The Secret Doctrine* (1888).
> Image-memory consciousness instead of logic, and the dense-air / thin-water young earth, are **Rudolf
> Steiner** (*Cosmic Memory* / *Atlantis and Lemuria*, ~1904–1911). "All is One / the Law of One" is the
> spine of both **Edgar Cayce** (1920s–44) and the **Ra Material** (1981–84). "Man is a star bound to a
> body" is from the **Emerald Tablets of Thoth** (Doreal). *All modern (19th–20th c.); none in Plato.*
> **Conflict to know:** Theosophy dates this to **hundreds of thousands / millions** of years ago;
> Cayce and Ra compress it to **tens of thousands**. (See Part IV.)

> **Sim hook.** This is the cosmology the *purpose* rests on: a universe that is one self-sounding
> vibration, and a pilot who is a fragment relearning the tone of the Whole. The "five realms" you tune
> are facets of that one vibration.

## 2. Society & the Schism — the Law of One vs the Sons of Belial

In the high age, Atlantis was governed by priest-scientists — initiates who held power and spirit as a
single discipline. Two paths ran through the people. The **Children (Sons) of the Law of One** held that
the gifts of the crystal and the body were a stewardship: to be used in service, healing, and the raising
of all consciousness toward the Source. The **Sons of Belial** held the opposite — that the gifts were
for appetite, dominion, and the self; they bred and kept a class of half-souled laborers, the **"things"**
(automatons — beings shaped for work and denied awakening), and they bent the sacred technologies toward
power over others. The whole tragedy of Atlantis is the slow victory of Belial's path over the Law of One:
not a war of armies first, but a war of *intentions* — service against greed — fought over the same holy
tools.

Daily life turned on the temple. In the channeled accounts Atlantis was a **theocracy of priest-scientists**
— initiates who were at once its rulers, healers, teachers, and engineers — and the great **healing temples**
were the civic heart, where crystal technology met medicine, learning, and rite. The seers describe a people
whose social, moral, and spiritual customs were ordered around attunement and the disciplines of
consciousness, with the crystal woven through ordinary life — light, power, communication, healing — while
much of the heavy labor was carried by the soulless **"things."** Beneath the high culture ran the widening
moral split that would, in the end, undo it.

> **Provenance.** The **Law of One vs Sons of Belial** schism, and the **"things"/automatons** used as
> slave labor, are distinctively **Edgar Cayce** (across ~700 readings). The **Ra Material** independently
> frames the corruption as crystal-knowledge becoming "involved with the governmental structure" and
> bending "toward bellicosity" (sessions 22.23, 10.15). **Theosophy** casts the same fall as **white
> magicians vs black magicians / sorcery** (Blavatsky). *All modern.* Plato's version is only a generic
> moral decline ("the divine portion faded"). The **priest-scientist, temple-centered society** and its
> day-to-day **customs** are filled in chiefly by **Frank Alper's** channeled *Exploring Atlantis* (1980s).

> **Sim hook.** This is your cautionary arc and your moral spine — **service vs greed, fought over the
> resonance tech itself.** The fall (§7) is what happens when the crystal is tuned for power instead of
> harmony.

## 3. The Great Crystal — the Tuaoi Stone / Firestone (the resonance heart)

At the center of Atlantis stood the **Tuaoi Stone**, also called the **Firestone** or the Great Crystal:
*"huge in size, cylindrical in length, and prismatic in shape, cut with six sides."* It was housed in an
**oval domed building** whose roof could be **rolled back** to bathe the stone in the light of sun, moon,
and stars at the most favorable hour; the chamber was lined with a **non-conducting** material (Cayce
likened it to asbestos or bakelite). Atop the crystal sat a **movable capstone** that both concentrated
the incoming rays and aimed the outgoing currents. It *"gathered solar, lunar, stellar, atmospheric and
Earth energies as well as unknown elemental forces,"* concentrating them at a focal point between the
crystal and the capstone.

From there its power flowed out across the land *"like radio waves,"* and — *"powered by these, crafts and
vehicles traversed the land, through the sky and under the sea at the speed of sound."* The same stone
**healed and rejuvenated** (initiates renewed their bodies and lived for centuries with youthful form),
**communicated** (it carried *"the human voice, and pictures, like modern television"*), and stood as an
**oracle of initiation** — "the means of communication between infinity and the finite." It was both
**engine and altar**: a power station and a doorway to the Source.

It did not stand alone. Atlantis ran *"an intricate matrix crystal grid system"*: great **Fire Crystals**
acted as central broadcasting-and-receiving stations, **pyramids** served as transmitters or receivers by
celestial alignment (one pyramid intensifying and beaming energy, another receiving it), and smaller
programmed crystals powered individual cities, vehicles, and homes. Crystals stored knowledge "much like
a computer," enhanced psychic sight, and were used for weather, agriculture, even **dematerialization and
teleportation**.

> **Provenance.** The Tuaoi Stone / Firestone is **Edgar Cayce's** signature contribution (the central
> reading is **440-5**, given 1933; he returns to it across the Atlantis readings). The six-sided
> prismatic form, the rolled-back dome, the capstone, the non-conducting lining, and power-beamed
> vehicles are all his. The **crystal grid / Fire Crystals / pyramid transmitter-receiver** elaboration
> and the seed-crystal taxonomy are **modern synthesist** material (e.g. *Crystalinks*, the crystal-
> healing movement seeded by **Frank Alper's** *Exploring Atlantis*, 1980s). The **Ra Material**
> corroborates Confederation-given "crystals and the building of pyramids and temples" for **healing**
> (22.21). *All 20th c.; nothing crystalline in Plato.* **"Tuaoi"** itself is Cayce's coinage.

> **Sim hook.** **This is the drive core.** The six-sided Tuaoi is already your Tuaoi-mode hexagon; the
> "communication between infinity and the finite" is literally your resonance-flight premise; the
> capstone that *concentrates in and aims out* is a perfect metaphor for tuning (gather the tone, direct
> the craft). The grid of Fire Crystals / pyramids = your temples, pyramids, and ley lines.

## 4. Flight & Vehicles — air-boats, life-force engines, and the light-body

Atlantis flew. The Theosophical seers described **air-ships** — *"air-boats,"* shaped like a hull without
a deck, first built of **thin wood** and later of a **light white metal alloy** like aluminium. They
carried passengers a few hundred feet up at perhaps a **hundred miles per hour**. In the earliest age a
pilot's own **vril** — personal life-force — drove them; later a **mechanical etheric force** replaced it,
generated from an apparatus amidships and exhausted through **two flexible horizontal tubes and eight fixed
vertical tubes**; steering was done by **angling the flexible tubes** to redirect the propelling force.

Steiner's seership gives a different engine for the same age: the Atlanteans *"knew how to put the germinal
energy of organisms into the service of their technology,"* **burning plant seeds** to release life-force
as motive power the way we burn coal — laying up "enormous stocks of seeds" — and their craft skimmed low,
beneath the mountain-tops, buoyed by the denser air of that era.

And there is the **Merkaba** — not a built vehicle but the *light-body itself*: in the modern teaching, a
field of **two counter-rotating star-tetrahedra** spun at the speed of light around the body, the
"chariot" (Heb. *merkavah*) by which consciousness travels **between dimensions**. The fall of Atlantis,
in this telling, was a botched attempt to build a *synthetic* Merkaba — to seize the light-vehicle by
force rather than earn it.

> **Provenance.** The **air-boats / vril / etheric-tube propulsion** are **W. Scott-Elliot**, *The Story
> of Atlantis* (1896, Theosophical clairvoyance). **Vril** as a life-force originates in fiction — Bulwer-
> Lytton's *The Coming Race* (1871) — and was adopted by occultism. The **seed / life-force engines** and
> low-skimming craft are **Rudolf Steiner**. **Cayce** has no winged craft of his own but says the
> firestone *beamed power* to land/air/sea vehicles "at the speed of sound." The **Merkaba light-vehicle**
> is **modern** — Jewish Merkavah mysticism repackaged for Atlantis/ascension by **Drunvalo Melchizedek**
> (*The Ancient Secret of the Flower of Life*, 1990s). *All modern.*

> **⚠ Honesty flag for the sim's premise.** **None of the lore describes true *space* flight.** The
> craft are *atmospheric* (Scott-Elliot, Steiner) or *terrestrial/undersea* (Cayce). The only thread that
> reaches *beyond* the world is **interdimensional** travel via the Merkaba and the crystal's "doorway to
> infinity." So the **"space sim" is mostly the game's creative extension** — and the honest, lore-true
> way to justify it is to frame your flight as **resonance/consciousness travel through dimensions
> (realms)**, with the Tuaoi as the drive and the Merkaba as the vehicle — *not* as rocket-style
> interplanetary travel. That is exactly what your 5-realm resonance drive already is.

## 5. Sound, Light & Healing — the Temples of Regeneration

Healing in Atlantis was done with **tone, color, and crystal**. The priest-healers worked in **temples of
regeneration**, refracting the crystal's light into the spectrum and the body's energy into color and
sound, matching a wound or weariness to its answering vibration. Long life was a *tuning*, not a medicine:
to stay in resonance with the One was to stay whole. Sound shaped matter; the right tone could mend, could
open the third eye, could carry a soul across the threshold at death.

The crystal was the healer's chief instrument. In the channeled temple-lore, **quartz crystals absorb,
store, and then discharge energy as a healing power** — the more facets a stone bears, the more it holds —
and a **set of smaller crystals is arranged on or around the body** in deliberate patterns to restore the
flesh or to lift the consciousness. Whole **healing temples** were built around these crystal grids, and
the priest-healers are said to have catalogued scores of ailments, each with its own crystal layout,
applying the same art to healing, to teaching, and even to surgery. To be tended in such a temple was to be
**re-tuned** — brought back into the body's true pattern by stone, color, and tone together.

> **Provenance — read this carefully; it bears directly on the sim's purpose.** The **idea** that
> Atlanteans healed with vibration, color, and crystal is genuinely in the corpus: **Cayce** (vibrational
> healing from the firestone), **Frank Alper** (the crystal-healing temples — his channeled *Exploring
> Atlantis* is the *source* of the modern crystal-healing movement's Atlantis origin myth), and **Ra**
> (crystals/temples given "for healing," 22.21). **BUT every specific frequency the sim uses is a modern
> overlay grafted on later — none appears in any Atlantis source:**
> - **Solfeggio tones** (396/417/528/639/741/852, later 174/285/963): invented by **Joseph Puleo (~1974)**
>   via numerology on the Bible's Book of Numbers, popularized by **Leonard Horowitz** (*Healing Codes for
>   the Biological Apocalypse*, 1999). **No** ancient or Gregorian basis. **528 Hz** as the "miracle /
>   love / DNA-repair" tone is Horowitz's.
> - **432 Hz** "cosmic tuning": a **modern** alt-tuning movement (vs the 440 Hz standard).
> - **Schumann resonance 7.83 Hz**: **real geophysics** (Winfried Schumann, 1952) — the Earth's EM
>   cavity tone — but its "consciousness" use is modern, and it is **not** Atlantean.
> - **110 Hz** "temple resonance": from **modern archaeoacoustics** (the Hypogeum of Ħal Saflieni, Malta;
>   Newgrange) — measured in the 2000s–2010s, **not** ancient testimony.

> **Sim hook + the discipline this implies.** The lore gives you full license for *"Atlantis healed with
> tone, color, and crystal."* It does **not** give you license to claim *"Atlanteans used 528 Hz."* For a
> sim whose real purpose is to carry resonant tones, this matters two ways: (1) keep the *in-world voice*
> to "the healing tone," "the crystal's note," "the resonance of the chamber" — never speak the modern Hz
> numbers or names ("Solfeggio," "Schumann") to the player; (2) you may *still use* those exact
> frequencies as the actual audio — just present them as the Atlantean tones they're standing in for. The
> tone is the medicine; its modern label stays backstage.

## 6. Geography & Structure — Poseidia and the Ringed City

Atlantis lay in the Atlantic **beyond the Pillars of Heracles** (Gibraltar) — in Plato, an island
"larger than Libya and Asia together." Its capital was built in **concentric rings of land and water**
around a central hill, ringed by bridges and canals, walled in bronze, tin, and the red-gold **orichalcum
that "glowed like fire."** At the center stood the temple of **Poseidon and Cleito**, and the line of
**Atlas** ruled. In the esoteric retelling, the later, broken continent's chief island was **Poseidia**;
its temples, and the **Halls of Records**, stood there before the sea took them.

Plato's measure of the city is precise enough to build from. Around the central island Poseidon set
*"alternate zones of sea and land... two of land and three of water,"* joined by a great canal *"three
hundred feet in width and one hundred feet in depth and fifty stadia in length"* cut from the sea to the
outermost ring, with tunneled passages wide enough for ships to pass beneath the land. The encircling walls
were sheathed in metal — the outer in **brass**, the next in **tin**, the innermost citadel wall *"flashed
with the red light of orichalcum."* At the heart stood the **temple of Poseidon and Cleito**, *"a stadium
in length, and half a stadium in width,"* holding a colossal gold statue of the god in a six-winged-horse
chariot, his head touching the roof; and the harbor was kept *"full of vessels and merchants... a
multitudinous sound... night and day."*

> **Provenance.** The **ringed city, orichalcum, Poseidon/Cleito/Atlas, "beyond the Pillars"** are **Plato**
> (*Timaeus* & *Critias*) — the one genuinely ancient layer, and pure political-moral allegory. **Poseidia**
> as the surviving island's name is **Cayce** (he also names **Og** and **Aryan**). The **Halls of Records**
> (three copies — near the **Sphinx at Giza**, in the **Yucatán**, and in sunken Atlantis near **Bimini**)
> are **Cayce** (reading 440-5); the **Bimini Road**, found in **1968** off North Bimini, became attached
> to a (disputed) Cayce prophecy that Atlantis would "rise again" that year.

> **Sim hook.** Concentric rings → your temple/ley-line layout; **Poseidia + the three Halls of Records**
> → discoverable destinations; the sunken capital → the "ruins" you can sound out by echo.

## 7. The Fall — when the tone was tuned for power

The fall was not a single sin but a forgetting. As the Sons of Belial's path spread, the crystal that had
been an altar became a weapon and a throne. The healers' tuning gave way to the engineers' overdriving:
the **Firestone was tuned too high** — pushed past its measure in the hunger for more power — and its
energies broke loose into the deep earth, splitting the crust, waking the fire-mountains. The seers tell
it in their own keys: **black magic and chimera-breeding** (Theosophy); **crystal weaponry bent to
government and war** (Ra); a **synthetic Merkaba seized by force** (Drunvalo). All of them are one story:
*a people who could hold any tone, and chose the tone of greed.*

It came in **three great destructions** (Cayce): the **first (~50,000 BCE)** broke the one land into
islands; the **second (~28,000 BCE)** — in which the misused crystals played their part — reduced them to
three (Poseidia, Og, Aryan); the **final (~10,000 BCE)** sank the last of them, Poseidia last of all,
beneath the waves "in a single day and a night" (Plato). Before the end, the Children of the Law of One
**carried the records and the knowing outward** — to **Egypt, the Yucatán, the Andes, Tibet** — seeding
the temples and mysteries of the age to come. And the souls of Atlantis, both paths of them, were promised
to **return** — to meet the same crystal power and the same choice again. *(That return, Cayce said, is
now.)*

> **Provenance.** **Overtuning the firestone → cataclysm** and the **three destructions / dates** are
> **Cayce**. **Crystal-weapon wars (~11,000 & ~10,821 years ago) and final inundation (~9,600 ya)** are
> **Ra** (10.15). **Sorcery / magician-war fall** is **Blavatsky**. **Synthetic-Merkaba fall** is
> **Drunvalo**. **"Sank in a day and a night"** is **Plato**. The **outward migrations** and **reincarnated
> Atlanteans returning now** are **Cayce** (Ra names refuge in **Tibet, Peru, Turkey**). *All the cause-
> and-crystal detail is modern; only the bare "drowned for hubris" is ancient.*

> **Sim hook.** This is your **endgame's shadow and its meaning.** The same Tuaoi you fly on can be
> "tuned too high"; the arc toward the Halls of Amenti is the Law-of-One path; the cautionary echo is
> Belial's. The migrations are your "seeds carried outward" — a natural new-game-plus / renewal frame
> (your crystal-renewal "warp to a fresh universe" already mirrors the Atlantean diaspora).

## 8. The Halls of Amenti & Thoth — the keeper of the records

Beneath the world lie the **Halls of Amenti** — the inner kingdom of the dead and of initiation, lit not
by sun but by the **Flower of Life**, a cold radiant flame from which the **Lords of the Cycles** (seven
great consciousnesses from the orders above) pour life into the world. There dwells **Thoth the
Atlantean** — priest-king of the city of **Keor on the island of Undal** in elder Atlantis — who by
returning to sleep before that flame renewed his body across **thousands of years**, outlived the
drowning, raised the **Great Pyramid** in Egypt as a hall of initiation and a vault for the records, and
wrote the **Emerald Tablets** so the way of Light would not be lost. His teaching is the Law of One in
practice: **turn toward the Light, raise your vibration, and refuse the path of the Dark Brothers.**

> **Provenance.** All of this is the **Emerald Tablets of Thoth the Atlantean** by **Maurice Doreal**
> (founder of the Brotherhood of the White Temple), written in the **1930s–early 1950s**. Doreal gave two
> competing origin stories (tablets *found* in the Great Pyramid in 1925; *and* received telepathically
> from Thoth) — i.e. it is a **modern composition**, and scholars note **H.P. Lovecraft's** fiction among
> its influences. **Important:** this is **not** the **Hermetic *Emerald Tablet*** (the short medieval
> alchemical "as above, so below" text attributed to Hermes Trismegistus) — different work entirely.
> **Amenti/Amentet** *is* genuinely ancient Egyptian (the West, the land of the dead); the *"Halls of
> Amenti as the Atlantean record-hall and Thoth's longevity engine"* is Doreal's modern syncretism.

> **Sim hook.** **This is your destination and your win-state.** The Halls of Amenti at the universe's
> center, the Flower-of-Life flame, Thoth as the guiding voice (your "guide companion" reskinned correctly
> — *not* an "AI," but Thoth / a priest-consciousness / the higher self), and the records as the prize.
> The "raise your vibration toward the Light" path *is* your "maintain resonance" core loop.

## 9. Consciousness & Ascension — the Stages of the Soul

The soul does not arrive whole; it climbs. In the Atlantean understanding, all of creation is a ladder of
awakening — a great octave the One sings to know itself — and every being is somewhere on the rungs,
rising life after life toward the Source it came from. A soul takes flesh again and again not as punishment
but as schooling: each life a chance to **meet itself**, to turn its debts and gifts into growth, carrying
its earned talents forward and leaving nothing truly lost. The rung where humanity now stands is the rung
of **choice** — the realm where a self, awake to itself, must decide which way it will serve: the path of
the One (service to others) or the path of the self (service to self, the way of Belial). That single
choice is the engine of the ascent; at a cycle's end the **harvest** gathers each soul to the realm its
choosing has fitted it for. Above the choice lie the higher realms — **love**, then **wisdom**, then the
**union of love and wisdom**, then the **gateway** where the many become One again — and past the gateway,
a new octave the old seers could only call mystery. This is why Atlantis presses on the present: its souls,
both the servers and the takers, are said to be **back in flesh in this age**, meeting the same crystal
power and the same choice a second time.

> **Provenance.** The **graded densities** are the **Ra Material / Law of One**: seven densities (1st
> awareness, 2nd growth, 3rd **self-awareness & the Choice**, 4th love, 5th wisdom, 6th unity of love and
> wisdom, 7th the *"gateway density, in which we once again become one with all"*), with the **eighth**
> opening the next octave. Graduation (the **"harvest"**) from third density requires having chosen a
> polarity — **service-to-others** (≥51% toward others) or **service-to-self** (≥95% toward self) —
> because polarity gives a soul the "potential to do work." **Reincarnation, karma as *"meeting self,"*
> talents that persist across lives, and the Atlantean souls returning now** are **Edgar Cayce**. The
> **three levels of consciousness** — a "first" (the Aboriginal/dreaming level), the imperfect "second"
> where humanity now lives, and the "third," **Christ / unity consciousness** reached via the Merkaba —
> are **Drunvalo Melchizedek** (paraphrased; his book is in-print). *All modern; the bare "the soul
> ascends through many lives toward the divine" is the shared spine.* **Conflict to note:** the three
> schemes do not line up one-to-one — treat them as three lenses on one ascent, not a single ranked scale.

> **Sim hook.** **This is the blueprint for your consciousness system.** Map your consciousness levels to
> the ascent (choice → love → wisdom → unity → gateway); make the **polarity of *how* the player plays** —
> service vs. self, Law of One vs. Belial — the thing that decides their "harvest"; and let rising
> consciousness **open the soundscape** (higher realms = more of the universe becomes audible). The whole
> climb is the long form of the **maintain-resonance** loop: stay in the tone, and you rise.
