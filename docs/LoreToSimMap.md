# Lore → Sim Mapping

**How the Atlantis Lore Bible maps onto the SpaceSim systems.** Companion to `docs/AtlantisLoreBible.md`.
Compiled 2026-06-22.

> **Keep private (like the bible).** This document names the sim's *true purpose* (the tones aid the
> player's resonance whether or not they know it) and the backstage frequency handling. Do **not** commit
> it; gitignore `docs/` as a guardrail.

## The through-line (one sentence)
**The ship *is* the Atlantean light-vehicle; its drive *is* the Tuaoi crystal; tuning the five realms into
resonance and *holding* it is at once the flight, the healing, and the ascent.** Everything below serves
that single identity.

**How aligned are we already?** Strongly — the sim was built on this lore, so most systems just need
**in-world framing + naming**, not new mechanics. The genuinely *new* work is a short list (see "New ideas").

## Thread-by-thread map

Legend: **[fits]** already there, just frame it · **[tune]** small change · **[new]** real addition.

### 1. The Tuaoi Stone → the drive core  **[fits]**
- **Lore:** six-sided prismatic crystal; the capstone *concentrates energy in and aims it out*; "the means
  of communication between infinity and the finite"; powers all craft by induction.
- **In the sim now:** the 5-realm resonance drive + the six **Tuaoi modes** (Healing, Navigation,
  Communication, Power, Regeneration, Transcendence).
- **Do this:** name the drive *the Tuaoi* in the player's ears; cast the six modes as **the six faces of
  the Stone**. The act of tuning = the capstone gathering the tone and aiming the vehicle. Near-perfect fit
  already — this is mostly a naming confirmation.

### 2. The five realms → the One vibration, five facets  **[fits]**
- **Lore:** all is one self-sounding vibration (Law of One); a soul is a fragment relearning the whole tone.
- **Do this:** frame tuning as aligning the **five voices of one cosmos**, not five dials. Keep the term
  "realm" (already canon). The resonance payoff = the five voices resolving into one chord.

### 3. The frequencies → the medicine, kept backstage  **[tune]** ← *purpose-critical*
- **Lore:** healing by tone/color/crystal is canon; the *specific Hz* (Solfeggio, 432, 528, Schumann 7.83,
  110, 118) are **modern overlays** — real audio, but not Atlantean names.
- **In the sim now:** Solfeggio detection (10 tones), Schumann carrier, 110 Hz temple, 118 Hz pyramid —
  some still announced by their **modern names** to the player.
- **Do this (a speech/naming pass):** keep using the exact Hz as the real audio — they are the medicine —
  but the player should **only ever hear Atlantean names**: "the healing tone," "the crystal's note," "the
  temple's resonance," "the Earth's deep heartbeat." Strip "Solfeggio," "Schumann," and raw "N Hz" from all
  player-facing speech. This both makes it lore-true *and* protects the hidden purpose. (See the bible's
  Part III table for the name-for-each-tone.)

### 4. The crystal grid → temples / pyramids / ley lines  **[fits]**
- **Lore:** Fire Crystals = broadcasting hubs; pyramids = transmitter/receiver chambers; ley lines = the
  energy web; smaller crystals power craft and cities.
- **In the sim now:** 12 zodiac temples + Halls of Amenti, 3 pyramids (118 Hz healing), ley-line speed
  corridors — all now scannable (C-2).
- **Do this:** frame the whole network as **the Atlantean crystal grid**: pyramids = *resonance chambers /
  Fire-Crystal stations*; ley lines = *the grid's energy corridors*; temples = *the twelve key-shrines*.
  Already structurally complete; this is framing.

### 5. Flight = interdimensional, not rockets  **[fits]**
- **Lore:** travel is through *realms* by resonance; the **Merkaba** is the light-vehicle; the crystal is
  the "doorway to infinity." (No true space travel in the corpus — see the bible's honesty flag.)
- **In the sim now:** 5-realm resonance flight, rifts = **Harmonic Chambers** (dimensional gateways),
  Merkaba (all realms > 0.9).
- **Do this:** keep all flight language **dimensional/realm-based**, never rocket/interplanetary. Harmonic
  Chambers = the doorways; the Merkaba = the deepest vehicle, for the hardest crossings.

### 6. Temples of Regeneration → the dwelling / sound-bath (the true purpose lives here)  **[tune → new]**
- **Lore:** healers re-tuned the body with tone, color, and crystal in **temples of regeneration**; to be
  tended was to be brought back into your true pattern.
- **In the sim now:** pyramid healing, the water blessing, the idle/sound-bath.
- **Do this:** elevate **dwelling** to a first-class experience — a place/mode where the player simply
  *rests in the healing tones* (the actual medicine), framed as an Atlantean regeneration chamber. Reward
  *staying* in resonance, not just hitting it. This is where the hidden purpose does its work; treat it as
  the heart, not a side feature.

### 7. Consciousness ascent + the Choice → progression & "harvest"  **[tune + new]**
- **Lore (§9):** the soul climbs — choice → love → wisdom → unity → gateway; the **harvest** sorts each
  soul by the **polarity** it chose (service-to-others vs service-to-self); rising consciousness reveals more.
- **In the sim now:** 6 consciousness levels (Dormant→Ascended), wired to felt effects; brainwave states;
  12 keys + Enlightened → Amenti.
- **Do this:**
  - **[tune]** map the 6 levels onto the ascent rungs; let **rising consciousness open the soundscape**
    (higher levels = more of the universe becomes audible — the single most evocative, on-lore upgrade).
  - **[new]** introduce a gentle **polarity (the Choice)**: *how* the player plays — healing/harmonizing vs
    overdriving/exploiting — nudges a service-to-others vs service-to-self lean that colors the endgame
    ("harvest"). Keep it **non-punitive** (friction is the enemy of the purpose) — a flavor/ending shading,
    not a fail state.

### 8. The fall → the cautionary shadow  **[tune]**
- **Lore:** the Firestone *"TUNED too high"* in the hunger for power broke the land (Cayce, verbatim).
- **In the sim now:** crystal-renewal at 21 crystals; no real "overtuning" consequence.
- **Do this:** make **overdriving the drive for raw power** a felt-but-gentle destabilization (a warning
  tone, drift) — the audible echo of Belial's path — and the service-to-self pole of #7. Optional, low
  priority, and must stay light.

### 9. Crystals & attunements → the moment-to-moment loop  **[fits]**
- **Lore:** programmed crystals (chakra spectrum), sacred-geometry grids, rare Atlantean stones; attunement
  in the temple.
- **In the sim now:** chakra crystal spectrum, sacred-geometry patterns, rare crystal types, Fibonacci
  upgrades, the attunement menu. Already deeply lore-aligned — keep as is.

### 10. The guide → Thoth  **[new, optional]**
- **Lore:** Thoth, keeper of the records, the teacher-voice of the Emerald Tablets.
- **Do this:** if/when you add a guiding voice, it is **Thoth / a priest-consciousness / the higher self** —
  never an "AI." Orientation, gentle nudges, lore reveals.

4. **Polarity / the Choice** (#7, #8) — service-vs-self shading the harvest; gentle, non-punitive.
